Chicken wrap

Description

Ready to try out this absolutely mouth salivating chicken wrap recipe? I mean just look at the image above!

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • Avocado
  • Paprika (spice to your liking)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Bread crumbs
  • Cooking oil
  • Tortilla wraps
  • Mayo + Sweet chilli (condiments of your choice)

Steps

  1. Butterfly your chicken breast with a sharp knife and spice it thoroughly with spice of your liking and salt.

  2. Scramble the egg in a separate bowl and dip your chicken in it.

  3. Add cooking oil to frying pan and put it on medium heat.

  4. Dip chicken in bread crumbs and place transfer to hot oil pan.

  5. Deep fry chicken until thoroughly cooked through.

  6. Transfer fried chicken from pan to plate cover with paper towels.

  7. Mix mayonnaise and sweet chilli in separate bowl.

  8. Take one tortilla and spread with mayo+chilli sauce.

  9. Cut tomato and avocado and place on tortilla wrap.

  10. Add Lettuce to the wrap.

  11. Take fried chicken and cut it up into medium-sized strips and add it to the tortilla wrap

  12. Roll that bad boy up and enjoy with some JUICE(it's mandatory)

And voila, you have created a masterpiece.
